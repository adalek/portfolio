Technology Temple Maze

Xuwen Dong & Lydia Yan

Supervisor: Michael Ang

December, 2021. New York University Abu Dhabi, Abu Dhabi

Visit Technology Temple Maze

Technology Temple Maze uses a combination of high-level applied machine learning techniques such as GPT 3, ml5.js, and VQGAN-CLIP to speculate a futuristic worldview that artificial intelligence has achieved omniscience and humans not only rely on technology to make big decisions but also view artificial superintelligence as the “new god”. The extreme worship of technology leads to the consequence that human culture and legacies are rewritten by artificial intelligence willingly. Instead of human paintings, algorithm-generated paintings are hung on walls; the predictive words from AI are viewed as the “new Bible” of humanity; the new golden record that is supposed to communicate the story of the human world to the extraterrestrials now only carries generated music. The project is divided into three parts: Technology Worship, Technology Temple Maze and AI Golden Record.

The player acts as a worshiper of the technology temple. The great technology will guide the player through the temple. The player needs to finish the tasks in order to complete the worshiping ritual. After the player play through the Technology Temple Maze, the player could visit the other two part of the project: Technology Worship and AI Golden Record
