Why Not Leave

A collaborative project of 6

August, 2024. Zhuhai, Amazing Seasun Games


Why Not Leave is an funny action-puzzle game where you get to play the nauty ghost to kick people out of your house.You will explore the different effects of items in the room and make the best combination of horror

Design process

Our design goal is to make the expereince both funny, exciting and stratigic.To achieve this, we designed three level of scaredness of the NPC with coresponded animation reactions. As for the numerical system, we categorize two types of objects, one with incresing vaule and one vise versa.
