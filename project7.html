Pulse



The project was inspired by[ Lifeline-Zeraphym](https://youtu.be/rVD2Aq4vc3I) and [Sturm I - Fear](https://youtu.be/v-RvlttIZFQ). The former using music and heartbeat sound to create an atmosphere that seems to tell a sad/hopeful story, the latter combines rapid piano with interest sound effects and play progressive melody repeatedly.

Making music is more like natural flow than meticulous design but I do want to show different emotion phases during the composition and to have a metaphor on life’s quietness, pain, struggle, strength, beauty and blossom. I am not able to fully develop my idea within the limited time but I tried to make a minimalist visual of the transformation of a single line to represent different emotional stage of life.
