XUWEN DONG

Dynamic Architecture (In Progress)

Xuwen Dong

Supervisor: Jung Hyun Moon

December, 2022. NYU Shanghai, Shanghai


Visit Dynamic Architecture (Demo)

Concrete objects such as buildings are considered as static and dead, however, the project wants to explore the possibility of this static object to be active and alive by applying transformation, shattering and clipping effects. Topics will be explored including repetitive and recursive geometry patterns in 3d, lighting,fog, and texture. The minimum viable product will be a big object made of multiple small cubes each of which can change position, rotation and scale to form different structures as a whole. The stretch goal includes putting the object in an ideal lighting environment and explore the shattering effect in different textures.

The main inspiration is from the video game Control where the building in the game is “alive” and constantly reshaping itself. The architecture style in the game is brutalism, which “usually constructed with reoccurring modular elements representing specific functional zones, distinctly articulated and grouped together into a unified whole. There is often an emphasis on graphic expressions in the external elevations “ (wikipedia). The other inspiration is from the SCP Foundation which is a collaborative fictional project that includes multiple supernatural objects and stories. The extended concept from this inspiration is that the movement and transformation of the architecture serves certain purposes. The architecture may expand, transforme its shapes, become invisible, collapse by its “emotion”.
