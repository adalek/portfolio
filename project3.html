XUWEN DONG

Dancing Pavilion

Unity Programmer, Art Designer -- Xuwen Dong

A collaberative project

Supervisor: Yunmi Her

December, 2022. NYU Shanghai, Shanghai

Watch full play-through (google drive)

In September 2022, a well-known tutor Du Yingzhe was charged with threatening female high-school Art Examinees to have sexual relationships with him in the name of tutoring them. Provoked by this socially sensational issue, we design a VR experience that simulates the journey of a girl, who grows up with enthusiasm for art, but after suffering from the tutorâ€™s sexual harassment, loses herself into a puppet. The tragedy was not an accident given that the phenomenon of corruption and abusive power is common in art school agencies. By comparing the girl to the puppet dancer on the music box, we want to depict a common dilemma of Art Examinees, where they seem to be free to pursue their dream but end up in threats and harassment.


Responsibility Distribution

Me: Make the end scene with shadow projection and animation. Draw the dancing figures. Import, export, cast, and record with Oculus. Help debug the project.

I participated in brainstorming the concept of the project and designing the details of the scenes. I provided style and color references for the project inspired by some games such as Little Nightmares. I designed and built the last scene of the rotating dancer being trapped in the pavilion, the metaphor of being a puppet is presented by the gradually appearing threads on her body and the weird background music. Since we encountered some bugs, this scene in Oculus plays though was somehow problematic so I recorded it in Unity to present it. I was also in charge of the Oculus setting to upload, test, and record the project. I drew the 2D pink dancing figures in the first scene and I helped debug some of the programming problems with other group members.
