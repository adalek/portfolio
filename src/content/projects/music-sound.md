## Overview

This section collects music and sound design work connected to live coding, audiovisual performance, soundscape design, and musical interaction.

## Pulse

Pulse is an audiovisual performance using TidalCycles and Hydra. It uses live manipulation of pre-written code to move through emotional phases of quietness, pain, struggle, strength, beauty, and blossom.

The project was inspired by Lifeline-Zeraphym and Sturm I - Fear. The former uses music and heartbeat sound to create an atmosphere that feels like a sad but hopeful story, while the latter combines rapid piano with sound effects and a repeating progressive melody.

Making music felt more like natural flow than meticulous design, but I wanted to show different emotional phases and create a metaphor for life's quietness, pain, struggle, strength, beauty, and blossom. Because of the limited production time, I could not fully develop every part of the idea, but I tried to create a minimalist visual system where the transformation of a single line represents different emotional stages.
